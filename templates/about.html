About This Project

My name is Daniel Shaanan, and this project was created by me for a problem i wanted to solve.
The problem i wanted to solve is that you cannot share playlists between music services, And this site solves exactly this problem.
for now the site supports only from Apple Music playlist to Spotify playlist.
Just login with your spotify account, pick a playlist name, description, privacy setting, and enter the Apple Music playlist URL you wish to exchange.
